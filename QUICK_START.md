- Select multiple words by clicking checkboxes

- Create groups to organize related words
- View groups at the top of Vault tab

## How to Use

### Save a Word
1. **Right-click** any word → "Save 'word' to Word Vault"
2. Add your understanding (optional)
3. Click **Save**

### View Your Vault
1. Click **extension icon** → **"Vault" tab**
2. See all your saved words
3. Use **"Select All"** to enter selection mode

### Select & Group Words
1. Click **"Select All"** (enters selection mode)
2. **Check boxes** appear on word cards
3. **Select multiple words** by clicking checkboxes
4. Click **"Group"** → Enter group name → Done!
5. Click **"Cancel"** to exit selection mode

### Delete Words
1. Enter **selection mode** (click "Select All")
2. **Select words** you want to delete
3. Click **"Delete"** → Confirm
4. Done!

### Filter by Group
1. In **Vault tab**, see groups at the top
2. **Click a group name** to see only words in that group
3. All your words are shown when no group is selected

## Chrome Web Store Publishing

**Full guide**: See `CHROME_WEB_STORE_GUIDE.md`

**Quick steps:**
1. Register Chrome Developer account ($5 one-time fee)
2. Create ZIP file with extension files
3. Upload to Chrome Web Store
4. Fill out store listing (description, screenshots, privacy policy)
5. Submit for review (1-3 days)
6. Your extension goes live! 🎉
